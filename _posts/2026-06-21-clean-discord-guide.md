### Modify Discord CSS Using Your Preferred Method
1. One of the ways of custom css discord is [openasar](https://openasar.dev/). It's not fully modded client, only the `app.asar` module while still have powerful custom css injector.
2. For this tutorial, I use openasar. Follow the install instructions given by openasar.
3. Go to [Disblock-origin](https://codeberg.org/AllPurposeMat/Disblock-Origin). Copy and customize your css.
4. Or use my [preset](https://github.com/ahmadsyarbini/ahmadsyar-scripts/tree/29fe3b8bbb7e2c94953b2c037e2cb0c91134450e/others/disblock-origin)
5. Open Discord. Click on the openasar at the left bottom (position may differ)
![discord1](/assets/images/2026-06/discord-1.png)
6. Paste your customized css into the theming
![discord2](/assets/images/2026-06/discord-2.png)
7. Press Restart
